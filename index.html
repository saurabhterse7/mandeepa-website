Founded in 2012

65+ Projects Completed

We, Mandeepa Realm Ltd—formerly known as M/s Mandeepa Enterprises—under the visionary leadership of our Founder & Managing Director, Shri Madan Satya Das, stand as a reputed and trusted name in India’s Toll Collection and Multilevel & Smart Parking Management industry. The organisation began its journey in 2012 with a focus on Parking Management Services and, through unwavering dedication, discipline, and operational excellence, steadily evolved into a leader in comprehensive Toll Operations.

Over the years, the organisation has successfully completed and executed 65+ Toll Collection Projects across India, demonstrating exceptional reliability, strong execution capability, and deep sector expertise. The company is actively associated with NHAI (National Highways Authority of India), multiple State Highway Authorities, and various Local Authorities, consistently delivering transparent, efficient, and technology-driven toll operations.
