About Us

We, Mandeepa Realm Ltd—formerly known as M/s Mandeepa Enterprises—under the visionary leadership of our Founder & Managing Director, Shri Madan Satya Das, stand as a reputed and trusted name in India’s Toll Collection and Multilevel & Smart Parking Management industry. The organisation began its journey in 2012 with a focus on Parking Management Services and, through unwavering dedication, discipline, and operational excellence, steadily evolved into a leader in comprehensive Toll Operations.

Over the years, the organisation has successfully completed and executed 65+ Toll Collection Projects across India, demonstrating exceptional reliability, strong execution capability, and deep sector expertise. The company is actively associated with NHAI (National Highways Authority of India), multiple State Highway Authorities, and various Local Authorities, consistently delivering transparent, efficient, and technology-driven toll operations.

Beyond national highways, the organisation has collaborated closely with the Airport Authority of India for airport parking ventures and partnered with Indian Railways for large-scale Multilevel & Smart Parking projects—further underscoring its versatility, innovation, and service excellence.

Today, the company proudly manages 20+ Toll Plazas under NHAI and 7 Multilevel Parking Facilities in Mumbai, supported by a strong, experienced, and highly skilled workforce of 1000+ employees. Its rapid and sustainable growth is anchored by a forward-looking leadership team and a specialised operational force proficient in toll management systems, traffic control, parking automation, and revenue protection.

Built on integrity, operational expertise, and an unwavering commitment to quality, Mandeepa Realm Ltd continues to progress with confidence—setting new benchmarks and further strengthening its position as one of India’s most trusted experts in Toll Operations and Multilevel & Smart Parking Management.

Our Mission

  • To be India’s most trusted and technologically advanced organisation in Toll Operations and Multilevel & Smart Parking Management, setting new benchmarks in transparency, efficiency, and service excellence.
  • To embrace continuous innovation by adopting advanced digital systems including FASTag-based solutions, AI-enabled surveillance, and automated revenue monitoring for greater accuracy and efficiency.
  • To uphold the highest standards of transparency, minimise operational leakages, and enhance safety and efficiency across highways and public infrastructure.
  • To build enduring partnerships with NHAI, State Authorities, Airports, Railways, and Urban Infrastructure bodies through consistent performance, trust, and operational excellence.
  • To contribute meaningfully to India’s smart mobility ecosystem with integrity, commitment, and a forward-looking approach.

Our Vision

  • To be India’s most trusted and technologically advanced organisation in Toll Operations and Multilevel & Smart Parking Management, setting new benchmarks in transparency, efficiency, and service excellence.
  • We aspire to lead the future of Intelligent Tolling through innovation in Automated Toll Systems, Fastag-based digital solutions, AI-driven traffic analytics, and real-time revenue monitoring. Our vision is to build a nation powered by seamless mobility, data-enabled decision-making, and world-class operational integrity — delivering safer roads and faster journeys.
